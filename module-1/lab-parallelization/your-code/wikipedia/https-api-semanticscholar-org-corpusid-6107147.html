Data Science and Prediction

@article{Dhar2013DataSA,\n  title={Data Science and Prediction},\n  author={V. Dhar},\n  journal={Philosophy of Science eJournal},\n  year={2013}\n}
  • V. Dhar
  • Published 2013
  • Computer Science
  • Philosophy of Science eJournal
    • The use of the term \'Data Science\' is becoming increasingly common along with \'Big Data.\' What does Data Science mean? Is there something unique about it? What skills should a \'data scientist\' possess to be productive in the emerging digital age characterized by a deluge of data? What are the implications for business and for scientific inquiry? In this brief monograph I address these questions from a predictive modeling perspective.\xc2\xa0
