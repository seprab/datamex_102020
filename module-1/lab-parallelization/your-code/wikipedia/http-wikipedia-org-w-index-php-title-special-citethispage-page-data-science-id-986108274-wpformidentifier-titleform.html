Citation styles for "Data science"

\n

APA style

\n

Wikipedia contributors. (2020, October 29). Data science. In Wikipedia, The Free Encyclopedia. Retrieved 00:22, October 30, 2020, from https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Data_science&oldid=986108274\n

\n

MLA Style Manual

\n

Wikipedia contributors. "Data science." Wikipedia, The Free Encyclopedia. Wikipedia, The Free Encyclopedia, 29 Oct. 2020. Web. 30 Oct. 2020.\n

\n

MHRA style

\n

Wikipedia contributors, \'Data science\', Wikipedia, The Free Encyclopedia, 29 October 2020, 20:56 UTC, <https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Data_science&oldid=986108274> [accessed 30 October 2020]\n

\n

Chicago style

\n

Wikipedia contributors, "Data science," Wikipedia, The Free Encyclopedia, https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Data_science&oldid=986108274 (accessed October 30, 2020).\n

\n

CBE/CSE style

\n

Wikipedia contributors. Data science [Internet]. Wikipedia, The Free Encyclopedia; 2020 Oct 29, 20:56 UTC [cited 2020 Oct 30]. Available from: \nhttps://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Data_science&oldid=986108274.\n

\n

Bluebook style

\n

Data science, https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Data_science&oldid=986108274 (last visited Oct. 30, 2020).\n

\n

AMA style

\n

Wikipedia contributors. Data science. Wikipedia, The Free Encyclopedia. October 29, 2020, 20:56 UTC. Available at: https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Data_science&oldid=986108274. Accessed October 30, 2020.\n
